Cards

There are 5 types of buttons. primary-btn, secondary-btn,icon-btn, link and floating-btn for primary, secondary, with icon, link and floating buttons respectively. 

          
              @import url("https://lucidui.netlify.app/cards/cards.css");
              <link rel="stylesheet"
              href="https://lucidui.netlify.app/cards/cards.css">

There are 9 types of cards.

  • text-only-card: card with only text
  • card-with-dismiss: card has cross button to dismiss/close
  • card-with-badge: card with a badge on top left side
  • basic: basic card
  • basic-textovermedia: card having text over image
  • basic-with-header: card with header at top
  • basic-only-buttons: card with only buttons
  • basic-only-icons: card with only icons
  • horizontal: horizontal card
